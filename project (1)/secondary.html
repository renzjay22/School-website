SECONDARY SCHOOL

Develop as a total person to grow in body, mind and spirit.

The High School Department is committed to prepare its youthful students with the tools essential for their development as a total person to grow in body, mind and spirit.

To ensure the delivery of excellence in completing the basic education foundation so that students are prepared for their prospective college career choices, St. Louis College Valenzuela has aligned its curricular and extra-curricular programs founded on the school's ideals of truth, wisdom and charity.

These programs are geared towards the further development of the inherent talents and skills of the students. The school seeks to mold their character based on Christian values, to prepare them for higher education, to realize the potentials of the student to become productive and responsible members of Filipino society
