COLLEGE

A Christian institution of higher learning.

"Wisdom, Charity, Truth" this is the motto of St. Louis College Valenzuela. With this as our guiding principles, the college carries out its avowed educational mission as a Christian institution of higher learning.

For over three decades, St. Louis College Valenzuela has formed and shaped young people who are imbued with Christian values who are productive and responsible members of society.

At present, St. Louis College Valenzuela offers a range of degree, diploma and certificate programs in pursuit of this mission.

COMPUTER SCIENCE DEPARTMENT

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

Associate in Computer Technology

The Program is designed to develop qualified computer professionals for the 21st century who are properly grounded and technologically savvy to meet the challenges and opportunities of a highly-digitized world.

The Computer Science Department is proud of its highly competent instructors, modern computer laboratory facilities and latest software. Our school is a pioneering member of the Philippine Society of Information Technology Educators (PSITE).

HOTEL & RESTAURANT MANAGEMENT DEPARTMENT

Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management

Associate in Hotel & Restaurant Services

The Program is a four-year course that aims to develop students to become entrepreneurs and executives in the hospitality industry.

Students are given a solid foundation based on a basic Christian liberal education with professional skills enhanced not only through classroom learning but also through practical demonstration and experience in an actual hotel or restaurant setting. Students will benefit greatly from the on-campus mini-hotel and professional kitchen laboratory. The school also offers a two-year associate in hotel and restaurant services under a ladderized curriculum. Students in this program undergo basic training in hotel & restaurant service, in response to the need for well-trained staff and personnel in the industry.

OFFICE MANAGEMENT DEPARTMENT

Bachelor of Science in Office Administration

Major in Computer Education

Computer Secretarial Course

The Program is a four-year course for future executive assistants and secretarial staff who are optimized for business record-keeping and communications in today's office environment.

Aside from traditional skills such as typing and stenography, students are taught and trained in advance composition and business correspondence. They undergo office practice training as well as personality development, public relations, and basic computer science. They also receive training in word processing, spreadsheets, and basic programming.

The school offers a two-year Computer Secretarial course under a ladderized curriculum. Students who take this course receive basic secretarial training for competency as future office personnel.
